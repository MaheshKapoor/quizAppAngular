Animal Flashcards for Age 18 months

Animal Flashcards for Age 18 months
Parents should show the animal flashcard to the child and make the sound in front of them. We should encourage kids to mimic the sound and recognize the animal.

Parents should let child recognize the animals before telling them. Sometime, they may recognize incorrectly but with regular practise they will improve.

Continuous practise of the flashcard may help to develop speech or improve speech in children.
