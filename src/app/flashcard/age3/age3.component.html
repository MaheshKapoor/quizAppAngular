Alphabets Flashcards for Age 3

Alphabets Flashcards for Age 3
Kids start saying some alphabets very quickly due the catchy sounds. They may not recognise all alphabets or the correct order at age 3. Parents should show the alphabet flashcard to the child and make the sound in front of them. We should encourage kids to mimic the sound and recognize the alphabets.

Parents should let child recognize the alphabet before telling them. Sometime, they may recognize incorrectly but with regular practise they will improve.

Continuous practise of the flashcard may help to develop speech or improve speech in children.
